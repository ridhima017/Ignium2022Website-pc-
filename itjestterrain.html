Rules



ELIGIBILITY :

This event is open for all departments.

EVENT DESCRIPTION :
  • Round 1: Aptitude,reasoning and logic based questions have to be answered in the given time limit.
    Prerequisite: Participants should be familiar with the online quiz system.
  • Round 2: A game of pictionary will be played where one of the team member has to draw the words written in the chit to make her partner guess.
  • Round 3: Participants should hunt for the clues in the library to reach the end of game. Fastest team to reach the destination wins!!!!

Note: Only teams qualifying a particular round will be sent to the next one.
